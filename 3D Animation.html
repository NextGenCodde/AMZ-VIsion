Since people often scroll through quickly, you need to try to draw attention to your products in the shortest amount of time. Since offers are often only displayed with product images, it is a good way to gain this attention by means of a product video (with Amazon EBC). Product videos are great for evoking the customer's emotions. Emotions that make the customer feel good and make them want to buy your product.
