The Difference Between Product Photographers & Amazon Photo Design

A product photo is not the same as a product photo, which is also ideal for Amazon. Whether you have your own online store, need a photo for a catalog, or trade on Amazon, the differences between these product images are significant. For this reason, it is important to know the differences between a classic product photographer and Amazon photo design

The product photo is the figurehead of your product and should therefore inspire immediately! It is a decisive factor that determines success or failure. Many Amazon retailers are therefore faced with the question of investing in a product photographer. We would like to explain to you here what differences there are between product photographers and Amazon photo design!

Differences Between Product Photographers and Amazon Photo Design!

Amazon's specifications for product images in the online shop

An Amazon photo must meet specific specifications in size and shape so that the product can also be ideally presented in the shop. In view of this, it is important to focus on product photography with a focus on Amazon photo design, because the product photographer should be familiar with the Amazon Style Guidelines.

Set product photography apart from the competition with Amazon

The right product photography can help you set yourself apart from your competition. Instead of losing your customers to the competition with boring or low-quality product photos, you can stand out. In order to know exactly how to best stage a product so that it shines on Amazon, you need a photographer who knows exactly how Amazon FBA works.

Optimal product photography with USPs

An important difference between classic product photography and selling on Amazon are the USPs, more precisely the representation in the photos of what your product can actually do and what special features it has. Such images Amazon are a must and should not be missing, so a photographer with the right expertise is needed.
